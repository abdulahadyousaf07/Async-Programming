First Understand the problem. Imagine you are making tea you have following functions:

    . boil_water()
    . wait()
    . add_tea()
    . wait()
    . pour_tea()

In above you do one task at a time 
Boil Water -> Add tea -> Pour tea
Everything waits for previous task to finish

NOW IN ASYNC PROGRAMMING 
 start_boiling_water()
    while water_is_boiling:
       clean_cups()
       arrange_tables()
    add_tea()

While waiting for one task , you do something else and CPU so not sit idle.
